## When to Use Automation ‚Äî When to Use AI ‚Äî Or Use Both (Hybrid Approach)

| Scenario / Business Need | Best Approach |
|--------------------------|----------------|
| High-volume repetitive tasks (data entry, scheduling, emailing) | Traditional Automation |
| Simple, predictable workflows that don‚Äôt require decision-making | Automation |
| Need for quick results and minimal cost | Automation (fast deployment, low overhead) |
| Need for personalization, customer behavior analysis, decision-making | AI |
| Customer support, dynamic workflows, lead scoring, forecasting | AI |
| Business processes that combine both routine tasks + decision-making + scalability needs | **Hybrid: Automation + AI** |

Often the most effective strategy ‚Äî especially for SMBs ‚Äî is combining both: use automation for routine parts and leverage AI where flexibility, data and personalization provide real value.
